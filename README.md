# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard

> An interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in **Power BI** to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, and workforce trends. The dashboard helps HR professionals identify the key factors influencing employee turnover and supports data-driven workforce planning.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Employee attrition is one of the biggest challenges for organizations. This project transforms raw HR employee data into an interactive dashboard that provides meaningful insights into workforce composition, employee satisfaction, and attrition patterns.

Using Power BI, multiple KPIs and visualizations were created to help HR teams monitor employee behavior, identify high-risk groups, and improve employee retention strategies.

---

## 🚀 Features

- Employee Attrition Analysis
- Overall Workforce Overview
- Department-wise Attrition
- Education Field Analysis

### Department-wise Attrition
The dashboard compares employee attrition across different departments including:
- Research & Development
- Sales
- Human Resources

This helps identify departments experiencing the highest employee turnover.

### Age Group Analysis
Employees are grouped into:
- Under 25
- 25–34
- 35–44
- 45–54
- Over 55

The visualization highlights workforce distribution and attrition across different age categories.

### Gender-wise Attrition
A comparative analysis of male and female employee attrition is provided for every age group, helping HR identify demographic trends.

### Education Field Analysis
Attrition is analyzed across education backgrounds such as:
- Life Sciences
- Medical
- Marketing
- Technical Degree
- Human Resources
- Other

### Job Satisfaction Analysis
Employee satisfaction ratings are compared across multiple job roles including:
- Sales Executive
- Research Scientist
- Laboratory Technician
- Manufacturing Director
- Healthcare Representative
- Human Resources
- Manager
- Sales Representative

The heatmap helps identify roles with lower satisfaction levels that may contribute to employee turnover.
